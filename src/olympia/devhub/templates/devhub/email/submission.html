Attract Users, Stay Updated, & Get in Touch

Add-ons help personalize the web experience for millions of users who have installed Firefox, and we want to help make your users happy! If you have not already done so, we recommend checking out these resources:

  • Help users discover and install your add-on by learning how to create an appealing listing: https://developer.mozilla.org/Add-ons/Listing
  • Want your add-on featured on AMO? Learn more: https://developer.mozilla.org/Add-ons/AMO/Policy/Featured
  • Join our mailing list to stay up-to-date with news and events relevant to add-on developers, including a subscription to our quarterly newsletter, about:addons: https://addons.mozilla.org/firefox/users/edit#acct-notify
  • Follow the Add-ons Blog to stay up-to-date on the latest news: https://blog.mozilla.org/addons/
  • For more add-on developer resources, please visit MDN web docs: https://developer.mozilla.org/Add-ons
  • Want to ask a question or file a bug report? These are the best ways to contact us: https://developer.mozilla.org/Add-ons#Contact_us
